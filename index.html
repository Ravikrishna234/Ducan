About Us

Ducaan is an E- commerce website which mainly focuses on selling only “Indian products”. Inspired by the “MADE IN INDIA” movement, we took the first bold step to develop this E- commerce website. We are committed to keep our profits minimal and help Indian Manufacturers, Indian products grow their industry.
Who are we?
We are alumni of IIIT Hyderabad passed out in the year 2020. We are very inspired by the initiative of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi ji “ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT”. We waited for the right time to serve our nation. At the present outset, we believe it is our responsibility to give back to the nation by helping our Indian manufacturing and goods industry grow their business.
Why Ducaan?
We are amazed to see such a positive response to the “MADE IN INDIA” movement. Considering the current lockdown situation in India, despite the strenuous efforts made by local dealers and manufacturers, they are unable to expand their business on a large scale. We connect to all of such local dealers and help them grow their business online through our website.
Why not other famous E-commerce sites?
Many other websites have products which come from all over the world including China. We need to exercise caution here while buying those products . Whereas in Ducaan we promise you that each and every product shown in our website will be made in India.
What can you do?
We ask you for your support by registering through our website (ducaan.in), which ensures that our Indian made products see a good market. The first step to achieve this self reliance is to support our Indian goods. Alongside, we expect to see the growth of our manufacturers on a large scale.
